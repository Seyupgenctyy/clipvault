ClipVault Local Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2024

Introduction

ClipVault Local is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection and Storage

ClipVault Local operates on a local-first principle:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No cloud storage is used
  • No data is shared with third parties

Permissions Used

ClipVault Local requires the following permissions:

  • clipboardRead: To read copied text
  • storage: To store clips locally
  • scripting: To detect copy operations
  • tabs: To monitor copy events across tabs
  • clipboardWrite: To paste saved clips
  • <all_urls>: To work on all websites

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Storing your clipboard history
  • Enabling search functionality
  • Managing favorites
  • Auto-cleanup of old clips

Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Enable/disable monitoring at any time
  • Delete individual clips
  • Clear all data
  • Set auto-cleanup preferences

Contact

Email: support@clipvault.local

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
